What is "endless"?

"endless" is the game name and it is a Guided Learning Project that focuses on games programming, designing gameplay systems, implementing mechanics, and implementing audio feedback to create an engaging 2D roguelike experience. This website documents and showcases development, reflections, playtesting results, and the finished product.

Guided Learning Project is a module offered to students who wants to take charge of their personal learning to broaden and deepen their knowledge and skills in a specific interest. This is an alternative to the other module called SCA (Sustainability & Climate Action).
